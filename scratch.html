Getting Started with Stargazing

Stargazing is a relaxing and fascinating hobby. All you need is a clear sky and curiosity.

Steps to Begin Stargazing

  1. Find a Good Location

    • Away from city lights
    • High elevation if possible
    • Clear view of the horizon

  2. Choose the Right Time

    • New moon nights are best
    • Late evening to early morning
    • Check weather conditions

  3. Bring Basic Equipment

    • Binoculars or telescope
    • Star map or app
    • Warm clothing and snacks
